ABOUT
-----
IFTV is a pioneering company in Vietnam that has developed "livestream real estate" — 
transforming livestreaming from a communication tool into actual sales infrastructure.
Rather than treating livestream as just "turning on the camera and talking," IFTV deploys 
livestream as a strategic component, tightly integrated with products, experiences, and 
real customer behavior.

CORE PHILOSOPHY (3 Pillars)
---------------------------
1. Rút ngắn khoảng cách thông tin (Shorten the information gap)
2. Tăng niềm tin thông qua minh bạch (Increase trust through transparency)
3. Kích hoạt quyết định đúng thời điểm (Activate decisions at the right moment)
